About AMSA

Our Journey Since 2020

AIU Myanmar Student Association (AMSA) was founded in 2020 by a group of 12 pioneering students who envisioned a supportive community for Myanmar scholars at Alor Setar. What began as a small initiative to help newcomers adjust to university life has blossomed into AIU's most vibrant cultural organization.

120+ Members
4+ Years Established
8 University Awards
3 Cultural Festivals

Through annual events like Thingyan celebrations and our peer mentorship program, AMSA has become a home away from home for Myanmar students. We've grown from helping a dozen students navigate campus to organizing university-wide cultural events attended by hundreds.

What Guides Our Association

Our Mission

To unite and support Myanmar students at AIU, fostering academic excellence, cultural preservation, and leadership development while creating a home away from home.

Our Vision

To be a model student association that empowers Myanmar students to become global citizens while maintaining strong ties to their cultural heritage and contributing positively to both AIU and Myanmar communities.

Core Values

  • Unity in Diversity
  • Academic Excellence
  • Cultural Preservation
  • Mutual Support
  • Leadership Development
  • Community Service

AMSA AIU supports Myanmar students at Albukhary International University through community, culture, leadership, and service.
